Π Paideia — Privacy Policy

Classical formation for the modern mind.

Paideia does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party trackers, and it does not send your information to any server.

What the app stores

Everything Paideia records — your learning progress, attempt history, streak, Seals, daily goal, saved cards (your Canon), and settings — is stored locally on your own device only. None of it leaves your device.

Permissions

If you enable the optional daily reminder, the app uses your device's local notifications to remind you to study. No data is sent anywhere to do this; reminders are scheduled entirely on your device.

Deleting your data

You can clear all stored data at any time from Settings → Reset progress. Uninstalling the app also removes all of its data from your device.

Children

Paideia does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.
