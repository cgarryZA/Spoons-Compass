Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 May 2026

Spoons Compass ("the app") is provided by Charlie Garry ("we", "us"). This policy describes what the app does with your data.

Summary

The app reads your device's location and magnetometer locally on your phone to point you at the nearest Wetherspoons pub. It does not send your location, or any other personal data, to us or to any third party. It does not store your location after you close it.

Data the app accesses

  • Approximate and precise location (ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION / ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION) — read in real time while the app is open, used only to calculate the distance and bearing to the nearest pub. Never transmitted off the device. Never written to persistent storage.
  • Magnetometer / compass heading — read in real time while the app is open, used only to rotate the on-screen arrow. Not stored, not transmitted.

Data the app does not collect

  • No accounts. No sign-in.
  • No analytics or telemetry.
  • No advertising or ad identifiers.
  • No crash reporting.
  • No cookies or local storage of personal data.
  • No contact list, calendar, photos, microphone, or camera access.

Third parties

The app does not communicate with any backend server we operate, and does not include any third-party SDKs that collect data. The list of Wetherspoon pub coordinates is bundled inside the app at build time from publicly available data on OpenStreetMap; no network call is made at runtime.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13 (or 16 in the UK).

Permissions on your device

You can revoke the location permission at any time from your Android device's Settings → Apps → Spoons Compass → Permissions. Without location permission the app cannot calculate distance or direction and will show a message asking you to re-enable it.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy we will update the date at the top. The current version is always available at the URL where you found this page.

Contact

Questions: open an issue at github.com/cgarryZA/Spoons-Compass.
