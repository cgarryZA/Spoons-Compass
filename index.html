Spoons Compass

The arrow points the way.

Android Download .apk

Free. No ads. No tracking. About 42 MB.

Open the app and a compass arrow points at your nearest J D Wetherspoon pub. The distance shows underneath. That's the entire app — no menus, no sign-in, no settings, no ads, nothing to tap.

How it works

  1. Your phone's GPS finds you.
  2. The magnetometer finds north.
  3. A bit of geometry does the rest.

Everything happens on your device. No data is sent anywhere. The list of pub locations is built into the app from public OpenStreetMap data. The app works perfectly offline.

Installing

Because Spoons Compass isn't on the Play Store, your phone will ask for permission to install it from a browser.

  1. Tap the download button at the top on your Android phone.
  2. Open the downloaded app-release.apk file.
  3. Android will ask you to "Allow this source to install apps" — tap through to Settings, toggle it on, come back.
  4. Tap Install. Done — find the app in your drawer.

Android Play Protect may show a warning that the developer isn't recognised. That's normal for any app outside the Play Store. Tap Install anyway.
